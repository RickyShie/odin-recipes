Lobster

Description

Indulge in a luxurious lobster dinner with our homemade recipe. Succulent lobster meat is cooked to perfection and served with buttery herbs for an unforgettable culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • Lobster
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Herbs

Steps to make delicious steak.

  1. Boil the lobster until cooked, remove the meat and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and add garlic and herbs, then add the lobster meat and cook briefly.
  3. Serve with lemon wedges and melted butter for dipping, for a delicious and indulgent lobster experience.
